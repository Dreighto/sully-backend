Headline: Hermes 4 14B at Q4_K_M lands at ~10.5GB total — identical to your current Qwen 3 14B setup. You don't lose VRAM headroom by switching. Coding + tool routing + reasoning at Q4_K_M of a 14B-class model is the well-tested sweet spot for a 16GB card; quality degradation vs Q5/Q8 is small enough that it's a non-issue for daily use.
